Cybersecurity Specialist

Job Descriptions

  1. Position: Senior Cybersecurity Specialist
  2. Type of Job: Full Time
  3. Salary Range: $140 000 - $200 000
  4. Location: Hawthorn, Melbourne, VIC
  5. Reference Id: REF001
  6. Successful Applicant Will Be Reported to: Chief Information Security Officer

About This Role

As a Senior Cyber Security Specialist, you will be responsible for proactively identifying, assessing, and mitigating cyber security risks, leading incident response efforts, and contributing to the development and implementation of our cybersecurity strategy. You will work closely with other IT and business teams to ensure a robust and resilient security posture.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor security systems and tools for suspicious activity, analyzing events and alerts to identify potential threats.
  • Contribute to the development and implementation of cybersecurity policies, standards, and procedures.
  • Possess in-depth knowledge of various security technologies, including firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, SIEM tools, and endpoint security solutions.
  • Collaborate with other IT and business teams to ensure security requirements are met.
  • Conduct root cause analysis of security incidents to identify vulnerabilities and prevent future occurrences.

Required Qualifications and Experiences

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • 7+ years of experience in cybersecurity, with a focus on incident response, threat detection, and security strategy.
  • Experience with security tools and technologies, including firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, SIEM tools, and endpoint security solutions.
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal, and problem-solving skills.
  • Relevant industry certifications (e.g., CISSP, CEH, CISM) are preferred.

Benifits

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Professional development and training opportunities.
  • A challenging and rewarding career path in cybersecurity.

Software Developer

Job Descriptions

  1. Position: Junior Software Developer
  2. Type of Job: Full Time
  3. Salary Range: $80 000 - $90 000
  4. Location: Hawthorn, Melbourne, VIC
  5. Reference Id: REF002
  6. Successful Applicant Will Be Reported to: Software Engineer Manager

About This Role

As a Junior Software Developer, you'll be an integral part of our team, contributing to the development and maintenance of our software products. This role is designed to provide hands-on experience and mentorship, allowing you to learn and grow your skills in a supportive environment. You'll be working alongside experienced developers, gaining valuable knowledge and contributing to real-world projects from day one.

Key Responsibilities

  • Participate in writing, testing, and debugging code under the guidance of senior developers.
  • Stay current with the latest technologies and programming languages, and be open to learning new tools and techniques.
  • Work closely with other developers, product managers, and designers to understand requirements and contribute to the development process.
  • Contribute to the continuous improvement of our software products and processes.
  • Assist in identifying and resolving software issues, working with senior developers to find solutions.

Required Qualifications and Experiences

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field, or equivalent experience.
  • A passion for learning and a desire to continuously improve your skills.
  • Ability to communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical colleagues.
  • Familiarity with one or more programming languages (e.g., Python, Java, JavaScript, C#).
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.

Benifits

  • A clear path for career advancement within the company.
  • Opportunity to learn from experienced developers and industry experts.
  • Access to ongoing training and development opportunities to enhance your skills.

Data Analyst

Job Descriptions

  1. Position: Entry-Level Data Analyst
  2. Type of Job: Full Time
  3. Salary Range: $90 000 - $110 000
  4. Location: Hawthorn, Melbourne, VIC
  5. Reference Id: REF003
  6. Successful Applicant Will Be Reported to: Senior Data Analyst

About This Role

We are seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented entry-level Data Analyst to join our team. As a member of our team, you will play a crucial role in supporting data-driven decision-making by assisting in the collection, analysis, and interpretation of data, identifying trends and patterns, and communicating findings to stakeholders. This is an excellent opportunity for someone with a passion for data and a desire to learn and grow within a dynamic environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in gathering data from various sources, ensuring data accuracy and completeness.
  • Perform basic data analysis tasks, including data cleaning, transformation, and validation, using tools like Excel or SQL.
  • Create simple charts, graphs, and dashboards to communicate data insights in a clear and concise manner.
  • Prepare concise and accurate reports summarizing data findings and insights.
  • Work closely with team members to understand data needs and contribute to data-driven projects.

Required Qualifications and Experiences

  • Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g., mathematics, statistics, computer science) or equivalent experience.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Basic understanding of databases and SQL (preferred).
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

Benifits

  • The chance to make a real impact on our company's success.
  • A positive and collaborative work environment
  • Opportunities for professional development and growth.
